To begin practice, choose the level.

Difficulty levels are based on grapheme-to-phoneme mappings. In a word like bit, there is a 1-to-1 mapping between graphemes and phonemes (bit /bɪt/). This word would belong to Level 1.

In contrast, you will come across a word like though at higher levels since it contains 6 graphemes but only 2 phonemes (/ðəʊ/).
